History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of technology enthusiasts in Silicon Valley. Over the years, it has grown into a global event, attracting thousands of professionals and innovators worldwide. Each year, we celebrate the milestones achieved in the tech industry and set the stage for future advancements.

Mission

The mission of TechCon is to inspire innovation, foster collaboration, and empower individuals and organizations to leverage technology for positive change. By bringing together thought leaders, creators, and industry experts, TechCon aims to shape the future of technology and its role in society.

Notable Past Speakers

Ada Lovelace: Renowned for her groundbreaking work in computer programming, Ada shared her insights on the history and future of computational science.

Alan Turing: A pioneer in artificial intelligence and cryptography, Alan discussed the ethical implications of AI development.

Grace Hopper: Known for her contributions to programming languages, Grace delivered a talk on empowering the next generation of technologists.
